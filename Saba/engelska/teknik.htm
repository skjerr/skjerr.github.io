Design

 


PAD1 is connected to the negative pole of one battery and PAD4 to the positive pole of the other battery.


The microcontroller MSP430F2011 from Texas Instruments is used for the intelligence.
This microcontroller draws only 2 micro Ampere when the lamp is switched off.


The lamp current is switched with an IFR7456 MOSFET transistor.


To make the plate as thin as possible the two IC circuits are turned upside down and placed in holes cut out in the PCB.
