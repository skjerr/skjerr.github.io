Installation

 

The brass plate is mounted, with doublesided foam tape, on the positive pole of the battery closest to the lamp.

The plate will then sit between the lamp and this battery.

 

The number of 1.5 Volt batteries can be 2, 3 or 4.

SABA needs a 3 Volts supply and therefore 2 batteries are connected between the printed circuit board (PCB) and the brass plate.

 

The top of the PCB, except the center elevation, is covered with doublesided foam tape and mounted under the negative pole of the second battery.

 

 

 

 

The thin lead between the brass plate and the PCB is drawn along the two batteries and taped in position. See the picture below.

 

Wind a rubber band, of suitable thickness, around the lower part of the battery closest to the lamp. This is done to prevent the battery from rotating when the lamp head is mounted.

 

 

Insert any third and fourth battery and then the 2 connected batteries. Assure that the lead goes clear of the lamp switch or other obstacles.

 

Turn on the lamp head and you are done.
