My masters thesis

A Method for Time-Domain Network Synthesis

Master of Sience in Electrical Engineering
Thesis by Staffan Kjerrström, approved by Lunds tekniska högskola 1967-11-24

The thesis

                

A known method for Time-Domain Network Synthesis was used as the starting point for the work.
Referens: Vasiliu C. G. ; A Practical Method for Time-Domain Network Synthesis;
                 IEEE Trans. on Circuit Theory, Volume 12 Issue 2 June 1965

The work comprised:
    Development of a program for synthesis of electrical networks with a given impulse response.
    Programming of the method in Algol 60 for the computer "siffermaskinen i Lund" (SMIL).
    Verifying the results from the program.
    Discussion of methods to implement the transfer function.

The program calculates:
    The transfer function of the network.
    Resulting impulse response.
    Deviation from the desired response.
